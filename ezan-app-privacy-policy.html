Your location data is used only within the application and is not stored or shared beyond necessary services.

Our application may request access to your device’s camera and photo gallery only with your permission.

This access is used for:

We do not access your camera or media without your explicit action.

When you submit comments, feedback, or images (such as boycott product reviews), this content may be stored and visible to other users within the application.

5. Usage Data

We may collect basic technical information such as device type, app usage, and diagnostics to improve the application.
